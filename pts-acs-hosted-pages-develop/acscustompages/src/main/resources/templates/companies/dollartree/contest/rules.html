2022 Dollar Tree Email Sign-Up Contest Official Rules

NO PURCHASE OR PAYMENT OF ANY KIND IS NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. PURCHASE DOES NOT IMPROVE ODDS OF WINNING. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED.

CONTEST DATES
The Dollar Tree Email Sign-Up Contest (the “Promotion”) is being held by Dollar Tree Stores, Inc. (“Dollar Tree”). The Contest begins at 12:01am Eastern Time (“ET”) on Sunday, May 22, 2022, and closes at 11:59pm Eastern Time (“ET”) on Saturday, June 4, 2022 (the “Promotion Period”). Entries received other than during the Promotion Period will be disqualified.

ELIGIBILITY
No purchase is necessary to enter or win. The Promotion is void wherever prohibited or restricted. All applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations apply. The Promotion is open only to legal residents of the contiguous forty-eight (48) United States and the District of Columbia who are age eighteen years old (18) or older and have reached the age of legal majority in their state of residence as of May 22, 2022. A person who complies with all the eligibility rules shall be referred to as “Entrant.” Employees of Dollar Tree and its subsidiaries, affiliates, licensees, service providers, prize suppliers, agents, advertising or promotion agencies, and those involved in the production, development, implementation or handling of Promotion (collectively, the “Promotion Entities”), and/or the immediate family (parent, child, sibling, or spouse) or household member (whether or not related) of any such employee are not eligible to participate in this Promotion. Non-compliance with these Official Rules shall result in disqualification from the Promotion.

Ten (10) winners will each receive one (1) $100 Dollar Tree gift card. Each winner is responsible for the reporting and payment of all state, federal, and local taxes applicable to the receipt of a prize. No transfer, assignment, cash redemption, or substitution of a prize is permitted except that Dollar Tree reserves the right, in its sole and absolute discretion, to substitute a prize with a prize of comparable or greater value or cash value. In the event that a prize cannot be awarded for any reason or a winner is not capable of redeeming a prize for any reason, Dollar Tree shall not be obligated to award any prize. All decisions of Dollar Tree relating to this Promotion will be final.

To enter, Entrant must complete an email subscription form online at https://dollartree-qa.ptsacs.data-axle.com/p/profile/search/contest/signup http://test.mailing.dollartree.com:8081/acscustompages/p/profile/search/contest/signup https://mailing.dollartree.com/p/profile/search/contest/signup . Entries must include all of the required information as set forth in the submission form and herein or will be deemed ineligible.

WINNER SELECTION
Between June 6 through June 17, ten (10) potential winners will be selected at random from among all eligible entries received. The odds of being selected as a potential winner depend on the number of eligible entries received. Winners will be announced by July 1, 2022.

HOW TO CLAIM PRIZE
Potential winners will be notified via email through email address supplied during the Promotion entry and will be asked to provide their full name, age, and mailing address within a specific time period. Prizes will be mailed within three (3) weeks of receipt of winner’s details. If a potential winner cannot be contacted within three (3) days of the first attempt to contact him/her, or does not respond within the timeframe stated in the notification, Dollar Tree may select an alternate potential winner in his/her place. Winners that cannot be contacted within the allotted timeframe will no longer be eligible to claim the prize.
